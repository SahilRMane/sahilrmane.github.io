About

I build the full loop — from raw sensor input to a model's decision to a robot doing something useful. My edge is that I've worked both halves.

Before my Master's, I spent two years at ZS Associates as a Decision Analytics Associate, engineering data-acquisition algorithms and real-time dashboards in Python and SQL. That taught me the unglamorous half of ML most people skip: clean pipelines, reproducibility, and shipping things stakeholders trust.

I'm now deepening the modelling half through an MSc in Robotic Systems Engineering at RWTH Aachen — machine learning, computer vision, and perception — building projects where models meet actuators and real-world constraints.
